Security Assessment

We systematically identify, evaluate, and prioritise security risks across your entire information environment — giving you a clear, actionable picture of your exposure.

Request an Assessment

What we assess

Our assessment methodology covers people, process, and technology — aligned to industry frameworks including NIST CSF, ISO 27001, and CIS Controls.

  • Current security posture against industry benchmarks
  • Asset inventory and attack surface mapping
  • Access control and identity governance review
  • Third-party and supply chain risk exposure
  • Security policy, procedure, and awareness gaps
  • Cloud and on-premise configuration review

Our approach

We combine automated tooling with expert-led interviews and evidence review. Rather than generating a long list of checkbox findings, we prioritise by actual exploitability and business impact — focusing your remediation effort where it matters most.

Every assessment begins with understanding your business context: what you protect, who the realistic threat actors are, and what risk appetite your organisation has.

Deliverables

  • Risk register with severity ratings and business impact context
  • Executive-level summary and risk heat map
  • Prioritised remediation roadmap with effort estimates
  • Framework gap analysis (NIST CSF / ISO 27001 / CIS)
  • Re-assessment option at 6 or 12 months to track progress

When to run a security assessment

Pre-funding due diligence, new product launch, post-incident review, ahead of a customer audit, or simply as an annual health check — a security assessment gives you an honest baseline to act from.
