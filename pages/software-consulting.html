Software & Tech Consulting

Architecture reviews, technology stack decisions, engineering team scaling, and fractional CTO advisory — for startups and scale-ups who need senior technical perspective without the full-time headcount.

Schedule a Consulting Call

Who we work with

Founders who are technical but don't have bandwidth to own architecture decisions. Engineering leaders who need a second opinion from someone who has seen the same problems before. Teams preparing for due diligence who need to clean up technical and security debt fast.

What we advise on

  • Architecture review and modernisation roadmap
  • Technology stack selection (build vs buy, vendor evaluation)
  • Engineering team structure and hiring plans
  • Technical due diligence preparation (pre-funding or pre-acquisition)
  • Security posture review and remediation prioritisation
  • AI/LLM adoption strategy and tool selection

Fractional CTO engagement

For early-stage companies that need senior technical leadership without a full-time hire, we offer a fractional CTO arrangement — attending leadership meetings, owning the technology roadmap, guiding engineering hiring, and representing the technical perspective with investors.

Our background spanning product engineering, security, and AI means we can cover a broader surface area than a specialist CTO from a single discipline.

Engagement models

  • One-off architecture or technology review (1–2 weeks)
  • Sprint-based advisory (monthly retainer)
  • Embedded fractional CTO (part-time, ongoing)
  • Due diligence intensive (accelerated timeline)
