Penetration Testing

Our ethical hackers simulate real-world attacks against your web apps, mobile apps, APIs, and networks — uncovering what your scanners miss.

Request a Pentest Quote

What we test

We cover the full application and infrastructure attack surface, tailored to your technology stack and threat profile.

  • Web application penetration testing (OWASP Top 10 + business logic)
  • Mobile application testing (iOS & Android, OWASP MASVS)
  • REST and GraphQL API security testing
  • Network and infrastructure pentesting (internal and external)
  • Cloud configuration and IAM review (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Thick client and desktop application testing

Our methodology

We follow a structured, intelligence-led approach that combines automated scanning with deep manual testing. Our testers go beyond CVE lookups — we chain vulnerabilities, test business logic flaws, and attempt privilege escalation to demonstrate real attacker impact.

All testing is conducted under a signed Rules of Engagement document. We maintain clear communication throughout so your team is never caught off-guard.

What you get

  • Executive summary suitable for board, audit committee, or customer presentation
  • Detailed technical findings with CVSS scores and proof-of-concept steps
  • Prioritised remediation guidance written for your development team
  • Re-test of all critical and high findings at no extra cost
  • Attestation letter confirming testing completion (for compliance or customers)

Compliance alignment

Our reports are structured to satisfy requirements for PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and HIPAA pentest mandates. We'll flag relevant compliance controls in every finding.
