What we build

We design and build data infrastructure and analytics products — from event pipelines and data warehouses to predictive models and executive dashboards — with security and data governance at the foundation.

  • Data pipeline design and implementation (ETL/ELT)
  • Data warehouse setup and modelling (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
  • Real-time analytics and event streaming (Kafka, Kinesis)
  • BI dashboards and self-serve reporting (Metabase, Superset, Looker)
  • Predictive models and ML pipelines for business use cases
  • Data quality monitoring and observability

Data security and privacy

Data projects touch the most sensitive assets in your organisation. We architect data systems with column-level encryption, row-level security, PII classification, data masking in non-production environments, and access logging as standard practices — not optional extras.

For companies under GDPR, DPDP, HIPAA, or SOC 2, we align the data architecture to your compliance obligations from the design phase, preventing costly rework later.
