What we build

We build AI products that go beyond demos — production systems that handle real load, real edge cases, and real security requirements. Our AI engineering practice is grounded in building and shipping, not just prototyping.

  • RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) pipelines with vector databases
  • AI copilots embedded in existing products
  • Agentic systems with tool use and multi-step reasoning
  • LLM fine-tuning for domain-specific tasks
  • Document intelligence and automated extraction pipelines
  • AI-powered search and recommendation engines

Why our AI work is different

We're a security company. That means every AI product we build gets threat modelled — prompt injection attack vectors, data leakage risks, jailbreak surfaces, and access control failures in agentic systems are things we think about during design, not after launch.

We've worked with the OWASP LLM Top 10 and MITRE ATLAS frameworks, and we apply that knowledge when we architect AI products. The result is AI that your customers and compliance team can trust.

Technology stack

  • Models: OpenAI GPT-4o, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Mistral, open-source LLMs via Ollama
  • Orchestration: LangChain, LlamaIndex, custom agent frameworks
  • Vector DBs: Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, pgvector
  • Infrastructure: AWS Bedrock, Azure AI Foundry, Google Vertex AI
  • Observability: LangSmith, Weights & Biases, custom eval pipelines

Deliverables

  • Production-ready AI application with documented architecture
  • Evaluation framework (evals) to measure and monitor model quality
  • AI security review (OWASP LLM Top 10 aligned)
  • Prompt management system and guardrails configuration
  • Runbook for ongoing operation and model updates
