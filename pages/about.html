About Koash

Built by practitioners, for teams who ship.

Koash Technologies is a managed software and security services firm. We exist because most security firms give you a report — we help you actually fix the problem.

What we do

We offer end-to-end managed software and security services — from penetration testing and security assessments to offensive red team operations, defensive security hardening, managed security monitoring, secure software development, and vCISO advisory.

Our team brings together offensive security expertise, secure development practice, and strategic advisory capability. We work with technology companies, SaaS platforms, financial services firms, and enterprises who need security done right — not just checked off a list.

Every engagement is practitioner-led. That means senior engineers on the tools, not just the scoping call.

Our mission

Security should give organisations visibility and confidence, not anxiety and overhead. Too many companies are left with a hundred-page report they don't know what to do with — or worse, a checkbox audit that misses the real risks.

We built Koash to be the firm we wished existed when we were on the other side of the table. Honest findings. Actionable guidance. A team that speaks both security and product.

We measure success by whether your security posture is genuinely better after working with us — not by deliverable count.
