What we build

We build conversational AI products that go beyond generic ChatGPT wrappers — grounded in your specific data, connected to your systems, and secured for enterprise use.

  • Internal knowledge assistants grounded in company documentation
  • Customer-facing support bots with escalation to human agents
  • Sales and pre-qualification assistants
  • Multi-turn conversational interfaces for complex workflows
  • Voice-enabled AI assistants
  • Multilingual chatbot deployment

Enterprise security requirements — handled

Conversational AI that touches customer data or internal knowledge bases has serious security implications. We architect every chatbot with user-level access controls (the bot only shows data the user is permitted to see), input sanitisation against prompt injection, output filtering for PII, and full conversation logging for audit purposes.

We've assessed conversational AI systems from an attacker's perspective — so we know exactly what to build defensively when we're on the development side.

How we ensure quality

  • Evaluation framework (evals) to measure accuracy and hallucination rate
  • Red-teaming the bot against adversarial user inputs before launch
  • Feedback loop integration for continuous improvement
  • Fallback and escalation logic for out-of-scope queries
  • Monitoring dashboard for conversation quality and usage patterns
