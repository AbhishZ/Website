Offensive Security

We replicate sophisticated attacker behaviour against your organisation — red team operations, adversary simulation, and attack chain analysis that tests your people, process, and technology simultaneously.

Talk to Our Red Team

What offensive security covers

Unlike a point-in-time pentest, offensive security operations simulate sustained attacker campaigns — testing whether your defences can detect, respond, and contain a real threat actor.

  • Red team operations with real-world TTPs (MITRE ATT&CK aligned)
  • Adversary simulation and APT emulation
  • Social engineering and phishing campaigns
  • Physical security testing (where in scope)
  • Purple team exercises — attacker and defender working together
  • Attack chain mapping and lateral movement testing

Why go beyond pentesting

Penetration tests validate individual vulnerabilities. Offensive security operations validate your entire detection and response capability. We ask: if an attacker established a foothold, how far could they go, and would you know?

Our engagements are scoped to answer specific business questions — crown jewel access, ransomware resilience, insider threat simulation — rather than generic checklists.

Deliverables

  • Full attack narrative with timeline, techniques, and impact
  • Detection gap analysis — what your SOC caught and what it missed
  • MITRE ATT&CK coverage map
  • Prioritised defensive recommendations
  • Executive debrief and technical readout sessions
